What we provide

  • 4 trips to India (5-10 days each)
  • Themes: context setting: product testing: partnering: market launch
  • 12-months of mentoring from an experienced entrepreneur with a track-record of scaling businesses in India
  • 12-months membership for your lead entrepreneur at the Sydney Startup Hub
  • 10 workshops with market experts to grow your business, improve your customer base and tailor your product to the Indian market
  • 2 launch/showcase events (one in India and one in Sydney)
  • Introductions to our network of investors, accelerators and local entrepreneurs
  • Introductions to Indian suppliers, distributors, partners, regulators and customers
